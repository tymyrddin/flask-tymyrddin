The Process

Submit a security concern and observe it pass through the five mandatory stages: intake, classification, routing, preliminary assessment, and closure. Each stage generates the appropriate documentation. Whether the underlying problem is resolved is a separate process, handled by parties not represented here, on a timeline that is not tracked in this system.
