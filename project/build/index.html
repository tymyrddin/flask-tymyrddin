Ty Myrddin

Security doesn’t have to sound like sorcery

I’m Nienke Fokma (sometimes known as Nina Barzh). I help teams and organisations focus on what matters in cybersecurity: clear risks, practical defences, and habits that actually stick.

Security should be a conversation, not a barrier. I work with NGOs, founders, and small teams under pressure to build digital resilience that fits their reality—not just the policy deck.

Whether it is mapping threats, untangling governance knots, or keeping systems sane as they scale, I bring decades of hands-on experience and a strong bias for the practical. No magic boxes. No vendor theatre. Just awareness that works.

My approach is patient and pragmatic, grounded in the messy overlap between human behaviour and technical systems.

My goal? To make security normal. Embedded. Understandable. And strong enough to hold when things get rough.
