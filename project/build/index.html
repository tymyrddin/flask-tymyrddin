Security doesn’t have to sound like sorcery

I’m Nienke Fokma (sometimes known as Nina Barzh), and I help people cut through the noise of cybersecurity to focus on what really matters: clear risks, smart defences, and practical habits that last.

Security shouldn’t be a black box or a barrier. It should be a conversation — one that empowers people, not overwhelms them. That’s why I work with NGOs, small teams, and mission-driven organisations to build digital resilience that fits the real world — not just the policy slide deck.

Whether it’s demystifying how attackers operate, spotting social engineering in action, or translating technical controls into plain language, I bring deep expertise with a no-nonsense approach. The goal isn’t just compliance. It’s awareness that works.

My approach? Patient, practical, and grounded in the realities of both human behaviour and digital threat landscapes.

My goal? To shift security from checkbox to culture — one informed, confident user at a time.
