About

Technically fluent. Organisationally alert. Situationally paranoid.

I am a security trainer, coach and consultant with broad experience in IT, OT and ICT. I work with role-playing and simulations where people can experiment, make mistakes, and learn in a safe setting.

My approach is inspired by Montessori (ownership and curiosity) and Virginia Satir (human behaviour in systems under pressure). I combine that with hands-on experience in digital and operational security, incident response, and complex technical environments.

Focus can be on resilience: strengthening individuals and teams so they can keep observing, weighing options, and acting with less loss of energy due to stress.
