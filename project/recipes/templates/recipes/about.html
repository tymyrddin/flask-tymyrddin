About

Technically fluent. Organisationally alert. Paranoid only when experience demands it.

I help critical infrastructure operators and tech companies build resilient security by turning attack knowledge into proactive defense. My approach combines simulated 'controlled burn' exercises, pragmatic secure architecture, and ethical threat research to move beyond compliance checklists.

  • For Critical National Infrastructure (CNI) Operators: I model sophisticated attacks through bespoke simulation to expose hidden risks in OT/ICS environments. This proactive "stress-testing" uncovers vulnerabilities before adversaries do, directly supporting NIS2 compliance and operational resilience (ISO 22301).
  • For technology startups & scale-ups: I build secure-by-design foundations that enable rapid growth without security becoming a bottleneck. This pragmatic approach integrates security into development, making ISO 27001 certification a natural byproduct of good engineering, not a disruptive project.
  • My approach is intelligence-led resilience: My work closes the loop between threat intelligence, realistic testing, and robust architecture. I find problems. And I help build the durable systems and processes that solve them.
