Security doesn’t have to sound like sorcery

I’m Nienke Fokma (sometimes known as Nina Barzh). I help teams and organisations cut through the noise of cybersecurity to focus on what matters: clear risks, sensible defences, and habits that last.

Security should be a conversation, not a barrier. I work with NGOs, founders, and small teams under pressure to develop digital resilience that fits the real world — not just the policy deck or pitch narrative.

Whether it's mapping threats, untangling governance messes, or making sure security doesn’t vanish under scaling stress, I bring decades of hands-on experience and a bias for the practical. No magic boxes. No vendor drama. Just awareness that works.

My approach? Patient, pragmatic, and rooted in the realities of both human behaviour and technical systems at scale.

My goal? To make security normal — not perfect, not heroic, but embedded where it needs to be.
