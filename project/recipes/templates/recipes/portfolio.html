Portfolio

See around corners

Practical explorations to anticipate risks, behaviours, and emerging challenges.

The Privacy Greenhouse maps how personal data flows, leaks, and mutates in everyday tech. Rooted in slow thinking and participatory threat modelling, it helps individuals and organisations identify threats, understand attacker incentives, and cultivate privacy resilience in complex, shifting conditions.

Red Wilds examines attacker behaviours, tradecraft, and persistence. Using a digital forest metaphor, it explores lateral movement, privilege escalation, and deception, helping teams understand and disrupt adversary rhythms.

Blue Beacon focuses on building trustworthy systems that maintain clarity, integrity, and resilience under pressure. Strategic defence, access control, audit-friendly logging, and actionable data flows go beyond compliance.
